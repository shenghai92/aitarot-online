Should I leave my job? What tarot can show before you resign

This question usually shows up when stress, misalignment, or exhaustion has been building for a while. A useful tarot reading should not only validate frustration. It should help you see whether you are facing temporary strain, a deeper mismatch, or a real season of transition.

Why this is such a high-intent career question

People search "should I leave my job tarot" when they are already close to a serious decision. They often need clarity around burnout, money pressure, identity, and timing. That makes this a strong conversion-intent keyword, not just a curiosity phrase.

What tarot can realistically clarify

Tarot can help reveal whether the role is draining because of a short-term phase, a structural problem, or a mismatch between your energy and the work environment. It can also show whether leaving now creates freedom or only moves the same unresolved pattern into a new job.

Better questions than "should I quit?"

Stronger prompts include: What is this job asking me to see right now? What would improve if I stayed a little longer? What needs to be built before I leave? Is this a timing issue, a boundary issue, or a true exit signal?

Why timing systems help career exits

Leaving a job is not only emotional. It is strategic. Astrology, I Ching, and Bazi-informed timing context can help frame whether the season supports visible change, preparation, repositioning, or cautious patience before a cleaner move.

When a Career Focus reading is the stronger choice

If the question is specifically about whether to stay, resign, negotiate, or reposition, a Career Focus reading usually gives more value than a general reading. It stays inside the work decision and keeps the answer practical.
