Privacy Policy

How privacy works on Love AI Tarot

Love AI Tarot is designed for vulnerable moments. Many people arrive with questions about relationships, silence, heartbreak, work stress, or future uncertainty. That is exactly why privacy matters here. We aim to collect only what is required to deliver the service and avoid turning sensitive questions into unnecessary personal profiling.

Effective date

Effective date: 2026-05-29.

What we collect

We may process basic access logs, the question content you submit, optional profile fields such as name or birth details, and required payment-status information for service delivery. Payment processing is handled through Creem. Membership access may also involve account email and access-session data because recurring plans require returning login access.

What is optional

Name is optional. Birth date and birth time are optional. One-time paid readings do not require login. This matters because not every user wants account-based usage for a private emotional question. We intentionally preserve a lower-data path for free and one-time reading formats.

What we do not do

We do not sell personal data. We do not treat emotional reading questions as unrelated ad-profiling material. We do not require more personal information than is necessary for the selected reading or membership path.

How data is used

Submitted information is used to generate and deliver the requested reading, verify payment or member access when needed, improve service reliability, and respond to support requests. We do not claim that optional birth details are required for every reading type. They are included only because some users want astrology or Bazi-style timing layers.

Your rights

You may request export or deletion of identifiable session data where applicable. If you need account or privacy help, contact support@aitarot.online. We will review the request based on the data necessary to provide the service and comply with applicable obligations.

Policy updates

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the site after an update means the revised policy applies to future use.
