What Bazi compatibility really means in a relationship reading

Bazi compatibility is not only about whether two people are a perfect match. It is about how their rhythms, tendencies, pressure patterns, and timing cycles interact over time.

Why compatibility is more than chemistry

Strong attraction does not automatically mean long-term stability. Compatibility asks how two people affect each other in daily life, conflict, timing, support, and stress.

Why timing matters in compatibility

Sometimes the issue is not that two people are impossible together. Sometimes the issue is that the current season is activating pressure, distance, or misalignment that would feel different in another life phase.

What users often want to know

They want to know whether a connection is sustainable, whether repeated conflict reflects a deeper pattern, and whether patience is wise or only delaying clarity.

How this works with tarot

Tarot can describe the emotional truth of the present moment. Bazi compatibility adds structure around longer-term fit and timing cycles.
