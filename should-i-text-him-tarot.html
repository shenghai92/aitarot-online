Should I text him? What tarot can actually help you decide

This is one of the most common relationship questions because the real issue is rarely only the message. The real issue is timing, boundaries, reciprocity, and whether contact would create relief or deeper confusion.

What matters more than the text itself

Before asking whether to send the message, it helps to ask what energy is already present. Is the connection warm, avoidant, blocked, unclear, or genuinely still open?

When reaching out can help

It can help when the connection still shows reciprocity, when the message is clear and low-pressure, and when the user is acting from steadiness rather than panic.

When reaching out may not help

It may not help when the silence is part of a larger pattern, when the other person has already withdrawn repeatedly, or when the message is mainly an attempt to end anxiety rather than create real clarity.

What a reading should help you decide

The best reading does not only say yes or no. It helps with tone, timing, intention, and what emotional result the user should realistically expect from contact.
