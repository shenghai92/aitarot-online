Why timing changes relationship advice

The same emotional bond can feel very different in different seasons. A connection may still matter, but the current moment may be poor for contact, apology, or commitment. That is why AI tarot Bazi relationship timing can be more useful than a simple yes-or-no reading.

What AI tarot clarifies first

AI tarot is strong at revealing reciprocity, fear, emotional availability, mixed signals, and whether the relationship is moving toward clarity or only circling the same confusion. It helps define the actual problem before timing is layered on top.

What Bazi timing adds

Bazi adds rhythm, pressure, and life-cycle context. It can help frame whether a silence phase is temporary, whether a relationship is in a cooling-off cycle, or whether both people are moving through incompatible seasons that make reconnection harder right now.
